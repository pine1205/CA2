Overview

Step into the neon-lit shadows of a dystopian future in Neon Eclipse, a cyberpunk sci-fi thriller where towering skyscrapers and sprawling cities are tightly controlled by a mysterious and oppressive government.

The story follows Nika, a brilliant hacker enlisted to work on a top-secret AI project. But as she digs deeper, she uncovers a chilling truth — the AI isn’t built to serve humanity, but to manipulate and control it. As Nika races against time to expose the conspiracy, she must navigate a world of secrets, surveillance, and betrayal, searching for allies while being hunted by relentless government agents.

In a place where nothing is what it seems, the fight for freedom begins in the shadows.
